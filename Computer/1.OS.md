## 1.3. 安装
### 1.3.1. 系统要求
- `SHIFT+F10` -> `regedit`
- `HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SYSTEM\Setup` -> 新建`项`：`LabConfig`
- `LabConfig` -> 新建`DWORD（32位）值`：`BypassCPUCheck`-`1`、`BypassRAMCheck`-`1`、`BypassStorageCheck`-`1`、`BypassTPMCheck`-`1`、`BypassSecureBootCheck`-`1`
